Media Library Notes

Import images and models into your Tilt Brush sketches by dropping your files into the Models and Images folders here. Images and models are accessed in Tilt Brush through the Media Library panel. 

Supported image formats: PNG, JPEG
Supported model format: OBJ



Notes:
- If models load with errors, flip over your painting controller for details. 
- Images previously located at Documents/Tilt Brush/Reference Images should be relocated to Documents/Tilt Brush/Media Library/Images.
